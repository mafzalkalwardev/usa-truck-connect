  • Choose Business Starter plan ($12/user/month)
  • Enter your business name and number of users
  • Create your admin account and complete payment
Google Voice

Step 2: Add Google Voice Add-on

  • Open Google Admin Console (admin.google.com)
  • Go to Billing → Subscriptions → Add subscription
  • Search for "Google Voice" and add the add-on
  • Assign Voice license to your user account

Step 3: Complete Setup & Select Number

  • Verify your domain (or use Google's provided domain)
  • In Google Voice, select a USA area code
  • Choose an available phone number
  • Link Voice to your Workspace user account
  • Test making a call to confirm it works
